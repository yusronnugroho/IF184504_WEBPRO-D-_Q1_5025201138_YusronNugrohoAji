Tulungagung, ꦠꦸꦭꦸꦁꦲꦒꦸꦁ , is one of region that located in East Java Province, Indonesia. There is so many culture in Tulungagung, such as Reong Kendang, Manten Kucing, Larung buwono, etc. A legend says that in ancient Tulungagung, there was a boy from Mount Wilis named Joko Baru dried Ngrowo River using a lidi from aren tree, if you curios you can search on it. Hoewer this region has so many tourist attraction form top of the mountains to beach on the south. Last but not least you can try all culinary in Tulungagung that festive form dusk till dawn.
